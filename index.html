Who is LadyBoom?

Well, my mama named me
Elisabeth Earley. I suppose
you can call me that.

I am a Software Engineer Student of Lambda School and member of Career Karma. I am the creator of the Legion of Boom | Dungeon of Soon, founder of codeKillas United, and a member of the FFT Squad. I am an advocate for truth, knowledge, equity, creativity, flyness and breaking into tech. I walk it like I talk it.

I am a Full Stack Web Developer, but specialize in all things Front End. However, I enjoy new challenges and am always open to broadening my scope.

I thrive in almost any environment, whether that be within an amazing in person team, or a remote setting. I am highly motivated and dedicated to completing phenomenal projects in the most efficient way possible. I would love to hear more about you and whatever you or your company are working on or developing. Feel free to connect, share and reach out to me. If you are looking for a wonderful addition to your team, please do not hesitate to explore my portfolio and contact me whichever way is most convenient for you. Enjoy.
